Nothing More

Nothing More is an American rock/metal band formed in 2003 from San Antonio, Texas. The current members are

  • Jonny Hawkins - lead vocals, additional drums (2008 - present), drums (2003 - 2009; 2011)
  • Mark Vollelunga - guitar, backing vocals (2003 - present)
  • Daniel Oliver - bass, backing vocals (2004 - present)
  • Ben Anderson - drums (2015 - present)

The ensemble has released five studio albums to date, including their self-titled album released in 2014. The lead single off their self-titled debut, entitled "This is the Time (Ballast)", hit number 1 on the Mediabase Active Rock chart and number 2 on the Billboard Mainstream Rock chart.
